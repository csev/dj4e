Django Data Modeling: Many-to-Many Relationships

django, data modeling, many-to-many, relationships, migrations, database

This lecture covers the implementation of many-to-many relationships in Django. It explains that one-to-many relationships are inadequate for scenarios where an entity can have multiple related items (such as books with multiple authors or genres) because they cannot handle arbitrary numbers of relationships.

In Django, many-to-many relationships are defined using the ManyToManyField in the model.py file. This allows models to represent multiple associations without fixed limits, ensuring the database can accommodate varying numbers of related objects.
