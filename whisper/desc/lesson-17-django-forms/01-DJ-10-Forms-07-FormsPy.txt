Django Forms: Validation and Template Rendering

Django, forms, validation, templates, csrf, post, redirect

Django forms are a critical component for handling user input in web applications. They generate HTML, validate data, and integrate with models, views, and templates. The lecture covers the form workflow, including data validation and error handling for create, update, and delete operations.

The lecture demonstrates the form processing cycle: generating form HTML in templates, validating POST data, and responding with redirects or error messages. It also explains the use of CSRF tokens and the form object's role in connecting templates and browsers.
