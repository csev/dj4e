Cookies and Sessions: Browser and Server Concepts

cookies, sessions, browser, server, http, django

Cookies are a browser-based storage mechanism that allows web servers to track a specific browser session. They are set by the server in response to a request and sent with subsequent requests to maintain state, enabling the server to identify the browser connection without persistent connections.

Cookies can be session cookies, which expire when the browser is closed, or persistent cookies, which have a fixed expiration date. The lecture illustrates how Django handles cookies using request.cookies and sets cookies in response headers, demonstrating the distinction between session and persistent cookies.
